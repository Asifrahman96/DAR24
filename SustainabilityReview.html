Why sustainability

Sustainability is no longer an option; it is a necessity for ensuring the well-being of future generations. As the world faces growing environmental and social challenges, we must recognize that continuing down the path of unsustainable practices threatens the delicate balance of our ecosystems and societies.

01
Urgency

The traditional models of excessive consumption and resource depletion are no longer viable. Pressing issues such as climate change, ecosystem degradation, and widening social disparities call for bold, systemic changes rather than incremental fixes.

02
Reimagining Sustainability

True sustainability goes beyond individual efforts; it requires a collective transformation in how we engage with the world and each other. This shift involves reimagining our economic models, promoting social equity, and establishing governance structures that prioritize environmental preservation and future generations.

03
Challenges and Rewards

Achieving sustainability will be challenging, demanding both personal and collective commitment, creativity, and a readiness to disrupt established norms. Yet the potential rewards are boundless: a healthier planet, thriving ecosystems, stronger communities, and a future of equitable opportunity for all.

04
The Choice is ours

We can either persist with business as usual or seize the chance to create a more sustainable and equitable future for those who follow. At stc, we recognize that true sustainability goes beyond environmental concerns-it’s about nurturing social well-being, driving economic growth, and caring for the planet. We’re committed to forging a future where progress uplifts all, ensuring no one is excluded.

Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), also known as the Global Goals, were established by the United Nations in 2015 as a call to action to eliminate poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that by 2030, all people live in peace and prosperity. The 17 SDGs recognize that progress in one area impacts others and that development must balance social, environmental, and governance sustainability. Countries have committed to focusing on the needs of those who are most marginalized. The SDGs aim to be embraced by all industries and sectors to address issues like poverty, inequality, and environmental degradation, while promoting health, justice, and prosperity for everyone. Many organizations set their own sustainability objectives, either aligned with the SDGs or tailored to their industry or operations. As a telecom company, we are committed to connecting people and driving a sustainable future. We believe technology holds the key to creating positive change, and we are applying our expertise to shape a more sustainable world for all.

At stc, we are committed to aligning our sustainability strategy with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), integrating them into our business operations and longterm objectives. Our sustainability programs - Caring for the Environment, Empowering People, Promoting Digital Innovation & Technologies, Improving Standards of Living, and Operating with Integrity - serve as a foundation for achieving meaningful progress in environmental, social, and governance (ESG) areas.

While we have made significant progress in these areas, we continue to drive forward with innovative initiatives, strategic investments, and collaborative partnerships. Below are key highlights of how our efforts contribute to specific SDGs:

1. Caring for the Environment (Aligned with SDGs: 6, 7, 13, 14, and 15) Water & Energy Efficiency (SDG 6 & 7): Implemented energy-saving initiatives across operations, reducing water and electricity consumption. Climate Action (SDG 13): Reduced carbon footprint through digital transformation and operational efficiencies. Marine & Land Conservation (SDG 14 & 15): Conducted beach and desert cleanup campaigns in collaboration with the Kuwait Municipality. 2. Education & Training (SDG 4): Youth Development Program (YDP) and continuous learning initiatives for employees. Diversity & Inclusion (SDG 5 & 10): Focusing our efforts to increase gender diversity and inclusion efforts to ensure equal opportunities. 3. Promoting Digital Innovation & Technologies (Aligned with SDGs: 9, 12) Technological Advancements (SDG 9): Invested in cutting-edge telecommunications infrastructure and smart solutions. Sustainable Production & Consumption (SDG 12): Signed a partnership with Tadwire to recycle e-waste as well as other waste generated from our operations in efforts to minimize our waste impact. 4. Improving Standards of Living (Aligned with SDGs: 8, 11, 12, and 10) Decent Work & Economic Growth (SDG 8): Recognized as the #1 Ideal Employer of Choice in IT & Engineering fields. Workplace Flexibility (SDG 10 & 12): Introduced 10 annual remote working days for employees, improving work-life balance. 5. Operating with Integrity (Aligned with SDGs: 10, 12, and 17) Ethical Governance (SDG 10 & 12): Established transparent policies for responsible business conduct. Strategic Partnerships (SDG 17): Engaged with government entities, suppliers, and industry leaders to enhance sustainability impact.
